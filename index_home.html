"Puppies are the best"

Puppies, oh, puppies,
I love these little fluffies.
They're cute, so cute,
The size of a little boot.
They cuddle, play in puddles
And love to look for trouble!
I want to squeeze them like a glove
And give them all of my love!
Puppies, oh puppies,
I love these little fluffies!

Words from Author:


I was inspired to write this poem to remind my human mommy and daddy that im very cute and loveable! So whenever I make a mess on the carpet, I show them this poem so they wont get cranky, hehe!
